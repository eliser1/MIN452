Our Beginnings

Cool Cat Cafe ™ is a fictional company established in 2020. In the United States alone, there are an estimated 58 million stray cats. Cool Cat Cafe aims to find loving forever homes for its residents. When customers visit and pick up one of our handcrafted beverages, they also get to visit with our cats, all of which are up for adoption. All cats housed at Cool Cat Cafe are fixed and up to date on shots, these procedures funded by our coffee sales and local donations.

How You Can Help

Visit our adopt page to see information on who is currently up for adoption. Donations may be made using our donate link or in store. Thank you for your support!
